Features

Receipt Tracking

Claim your deductions in full

Make tax time a breeze with a summary of all your giving in one place. Just email us your donation receipts in as they arrive and we'll do the rest.

Give To Your Plan

Track your giving progress

Set the causes that matter to you and your annual giving target. Our reports will show how much you've given across your different causes.

Matched Giving Alerts

Multiply your impact

If you like, we can let you know about current matched giving opportunities for causes you've supported.

DonorGuard
